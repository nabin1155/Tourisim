Why Nomadic Experience

Nomads have their own ways of deciding among themselves who should go, where, for how long, and why. And when they return, they share those resources broadly within the community. And there always remains the banner or social covering. Their clan and tribal community membership and mutual loyalty is never superficial or temporary.
